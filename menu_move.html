Change the Menu's position/selection

There are two types of movement function:

  • Those that change the selection:
    • menu_top_page()
    • menu_middle_page()
    • menu_bottom_page()
    • menu_prev_entry()
    • menu_next_entry()
    • menu_first_entry()
    • menu_last_entry()
  • Those that change the view:
    • menu_current_top()
    • menu_current_middle()
    • menu_current_bottom()
    • menu_half_up()
    • menu_half_down()
    • menu_prev_line()
    • menu_next_line()
    • menu_prev_page()
    • menu_next_page()

Changing the selection may cause the view to move and vice versa.
